1. **0G RPC** (`https://evmrpc.0g.ai`) - Blockchain data
2. **DefiLlama API** (`https://api.llama.fi`) - DeFi metrics
3. **Groq API** (`https://api.groq.com`) - AI inference (Llama 3.1)
